# 🧭 Leader's Compass (Prototype)

**Casual Conversation → Research-Based Diagnosis**

Leader’s Compass is a lightweight, browser-based tool designed to help leaders understand their team — without formal surveys, without bias, and without triggering defensive responses.

Instead of asking employees to fill forms, this tool turns **informal conversations** into structured organizational insights.

---

## 💡 Why This Exists

Too many organizations claim to "develop people"…  
while quietly damaging their motivation.

Employees are often:
- given more responsibility  
- without recognition  
- without clarity  
- without fair compensation  

This is not development.  
This is **hidden exploitation**.

Leader’s Compass was built to **detect and prevent that pattern early**.

---

## 🧠 Core Frameworks

This tool integrates multiple research-backed models:

- **Job Characteristics Model (JCM)**  
  → Skill Variety, Autonomy, Task Identity, Significance, Feedback  

- **Job Demands–Resources Model (JD-R)**  
  → Workload, Support, Psychological Safety, Role Clarity  

- **Self-Determination Theory (SDT)**  
  → Growth & intrinsic motivation  

- Burnout & Engagement Indicators  
  → Inspired by Maslach & Schaufeli models  

---

## 🔍 Key Features

- 🎯 **Invisible Assessment**  
  Diagnose team conditions through casual conversations (no formal survey)

- 📊 **10-Dimension Radar Profile**  
  Visualize motivation, stress, and work design

- ⚠️ **Automated Diagnosis**  
  Detect burnout, boreout, role ambiguity, low psychological safety, etc.

- 🧭 **Action-Oriented Recommendations**  
  Not just data — but what leaders should actually do

- ⭐ **Forced Recognition System**  
  You cannot assign new responsibility without committing to recognition

---

## ⚖️ Ethical Principle

> If you assign more responsibility, you must commit to recognition.  
> Otherwise, don’t call it leadership.
